 Description

Tragic Sans is a text-based, vertical, platformer, where the player navigates a text document as the embodiment of the font Comic Sans on a journey to find happiness.

 History

Tragic Sans was created for the Original Game Project of the Spring 2018 Section of CTIN 483 at USC. 

The development took place during April and early May of 2018. It was the first game that developers Tom Devereaux and Sully Zack worked on together, and was a learning experience for them both as they furthered their skills with Unity.

The inspiration for the project, unironically, came from the Emoji Movie, and popular "Tag Yourself Memes."
